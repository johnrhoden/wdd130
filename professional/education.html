Education

I enrolled at Brigham Young University - Provo as a freshman. While an undergrad, I participated in ROTC and Washington Seminar. Before my junior year, I was offered a full-time job and postponed school for several years to work in politics. Eventually, I finished my degree by taking courses from a local community college in Virginia and through BYU online. In August 2014, I graduated from BYU with a bachelors in general studies, with an emphasis in American studies.

In Augut 2015, I returned to Provo to earn my Masters in Business Administration from Brigham Young University. While there, I interned at Blue Raven Solar and served as the president of the BYU Veterans club. While I struggled my first year to secure an internship, I was blessed in my second year to receive 6 full-time job offers to Amazon, Johnson and Johnson, Delta Airlines, Union Pacific, Blue Raven Solar, and T-Mobile. In April 2017, I graduated with my MBA with minors in analytics and information systems.

In September 2022, I enrolled in BYU Pathways to pursue a certificate in Web and Computer Programing.I work in digital product management and am pursuing this certificate to increase my technical acumen. So far, I've been very pleased with the instruction, instructor, tools, TAs, and resources.
